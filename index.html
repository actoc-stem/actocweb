Who We Are

Student-founded, youth-led nonprofit ensuring every child has access to free STEM and wellness education

Founded by High School Students

ACTOC was created by Nitika Metharamitta & Amulya Atluri because we saw the gaps in educational access firsthand. We're not a large institution - we're young people committed to making real change, starting in our own community.

The Barriers We Remove

Multiple interconnected obstacles prevent children from accessing STEM and wellness education

STEM Access Gap

  • School budget limitations
  • Family financial barriers
  • Geographic limitations
  • Lack of awareness

Wellness Education Gap

  • Underfunded programs
  • Mental health stigma
  • No age-appropriate resources
  • Cultural barriers

Our Solution

We remove barriers completely with free, accessible programs

STEM & Robotics Workshops

Hands-on robotics and STEM workshops for elementary students. We bring everything - no cost to schools or families.

  • Completely free
  • Hands-on learning
  • No prior experience needed
